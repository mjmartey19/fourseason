Sydney’s Premier Tennis School in the Inner West and Inner City

For Adults’ and Children’s Tennis Programs

If you want to learn to play tennis in Sydney, or continue to improve your tennis game, Four Seasons Tennis School provides a range of tennis programs enjoyed by its 700 student players. Whether beginner or advanced, child or adult we offer group coaching, social tennis, competitions, private coaching, tournaments, weekday morning programs for ladies/mothers, and kids school holiday programs at Marrickville Tennis Club and Wicks Park Tennis Centre in Marrickville.

Professional Coaches

The school's core coaches are certified by the Australian Tennis Professional Coaches Association /Tennis Australia.

Relaxed environment/ Great locations

Convenient and easy to find location at Marrickville Tennis Club and Wicks Park Tennis Centre in Marrickville

Internationally recognized

With highly-qualified home-grown and overseas coaches we are able to give our students a broad spectrum of instruction.

Over 20 years experience

Our School is in its 20th year of teaching adults and children the secrets and marvels of tennis.
